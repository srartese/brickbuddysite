THE STORY:

What happens when there is no more room for bricks in brick city?


Spring 2017 Graduation. Your parents buy you a brick to be placed on RIT's quarter mile and there is one issue, RIT says your brick just cannot stay. Your brick does not have a spot with all the other bricks and is now homeless. You go to sleep that night and dream of your poor brick.....
